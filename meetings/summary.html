Executive Summary

Initial sales call with Ignacio Guerra, Financial Director at SIALNE (a poultry company in Brazil, part of the Carnero family group). The team presented their three-phase AI automation approach (Augment, Automate, Agentize) and demonstrated a live bank reconciliation tool achieving ~90% automatic matching using 35 business rules. Ignacio expressed strong interest in starting with bank reconciliation as a quick-win pilot project. Additional opportunities discussed included legal document management with role-based access controls and SAP security governance. Ignacio will validate the reconciliation use case internally with his accounting team before scheduling a follow-up.

Discussion Points

  • 1Bank Reconciliation Automation

    Live demo of the bank reconciliation tool. The system uploads bank statements and ERP ledger entries (5,800+ records), applies ~35 matching rules in order of confidence (exact match on ID/amount/date, then fuzzy matches with date/amount tolerance), achieving ~90% automatic reconciliation. Currently outputs to Excel but can be integrated further. The prospect's team currently spends 2–3 days with 1–2 people on this monthly process.

  • 2Three-Phase AI Implementation Approach

    Methodology: (1) Augment — point automations to help existing staff do more; (2) Automate — full process automation with API integrations replacing manual Excel workflows; (3) Agentize — deploy autonomous AI agents so staff shift from operators to supervisors. Emphasized that jumping directly to agents often fails due to organizational readiness.

  • 3SIALNE Organizational Structure & IT Governance

    SIALNE is the anchor company of the Carnero family group (poultry), with a smaller dairy subsidiary. IT governance follows an annual planning process: business directors present tech needs, IT allocates internal resources (~15 people), and overflow is outsourced with the requesting area paying from its own budget. IT acts as PMO for third-party projects.

  • 4Legal Document Management & Access Control

    Ignacio's legal team wants a contract management system accessible via WhatsApp. Critical challenge: role-based access at field level (e.g., one user sees contract duration but not pricing). The team presented their existing Legal Hub with contract templates, approval workflows, and queryable database.

  • 5SAP Security & Access Governance

    SIALNE recently upgraded to SAP S/4HANA but lacks formal access governance. Access requests are granted informally. As the company scales, they need proper profile definitions, review routines, and usage monitoring. Ignacio asked for vendor recommendations.

  • 6Organizational Diagnostic Offering

    Diagnostic service maps the organization through interviews and WhatsApp conversations. Deliverables include: digital maturity assessment, economic opportunity analysis, macro-process documentation, knowledge gap identification, system criticality prioritization, and ROI-driven implementation recommendations.

  • 7WhatsApp Integration for Business Processes

    Routing IT service requests and invoice processing through WhatsApp instead of intranet-based ticketing. Example: photograph an invoice via WhatsApp, auto-identify in SAP, and process without needing someone at a computer.

  • 8Competitive Landscape

    SIALNE also spoke with Celonis about similar services. They are evaluating multiple vendors to find the best fit per specific need in terms of quality, cost, and speed.
