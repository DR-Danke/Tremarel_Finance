B2B Inbound Conversion & Traffic Analytics
Reviewed website traffic of 53,400 monthly visitors and 35,100 active users (30-day). The B2B inbound conversion rate sits at 0.39%, generating 17 bookings and only 3 qualified leads per month. This was identified as the primary growth opportunity.
Outbound Cost Structure & CAC Analysis
Analyzed outbound customer acquisition costs: $519 CAC per client, $7 per meeting booked, $17 per qualified meeting, and $98 per proposal sent. No paid advertising is currently used, representing an untapped growth channel.
Engagement Proposal & Financial Projections
Two engagement options were proposed totaling approximately $19,000, with models including fixed cost and revenue share. Revenue projections target $1.7-2M in year 1 and $4M in year 2.
Automation Infrastructure (Gumloop)
Arkangel operates 162 automated workflows on Gumloop, powering their operational processes. Onboarding duration is 5 days.
Networking Events Strategy
Networking dinners are planned for Miami, Boston, and NYC starting March. Need to add follow-up forms to improve tracking and conversion from events.
Newsletter & UGC Content
The UGC newsletter performs well when active but has been inconsistent. Reactivation with a consistent cadence was discussed as a priority.
