Discussion Points

1. Bank Reconciliation Automation
Live demo of the reconciliation platform showing how 5,800+ bank records are matched against ERP accounting entries using ~35 progressive rules (exact match first, then fuzzy matching on dates/amounts). Achieves ~90% automatic match rate, reducing a 2-3 day manual process to minutes. Output delivered as Excel initially, with platform-based workflow available as a next step.
2. Three-Phase AI Implementation Methodology
Presented the Augment-Automate-Agentize framework: (1) Augment gives existing teams tools to do more with point automations, (2) Automate connects full processes via API integrations eliminating manual Excel transfers, (3) Agentize deploys autonomous AI agents that run processes end-to-end with humans as supervisors instead of operators.
3. Cialne Organizational Structure & IT Governance
Cialne is the anchor company of the Carnero family group (also includes a dairy company and a family office). The CFO oversees Finance, IT (~15 people), Legal, and Facilities. IT projects are prioritized annually via an IT committee where each business director presents technology needs. When internal capacity is insufficient, they outsource with IT acting as PMO.
4. Organizational Diagnostics Offering
Presented the diagnostic process that maps organizational processes through WhatsApp interviews, identifies bottlenecks, assesses digital maturity per department, documents knowledge gaps, analyzes system integrations and technical debt, and produces ROI-driven recommendations for prioritized investments.
5. Legal Hub & RAG-Based Document Access
Discussed a contract management system where users fill templates, legal department approves, and contracts become queryable in a centralized database. Introduced the RAG concept for role-based information access across departments (legal, HR, clients, vendors) - aligning with Cialne's legal team's desire for a WhatsApp-based contract query system with granular access control.
6. SAP Security & Access Governance
Cialne recently upgraded to SAP S/4HANA but lacks access governance processes. Currently ~1,000 users with ad-hoc access grants. They need profile definition by process/user type, access review routines, and activity measurement. Asked for vendor recommendations from Colombia to compare with Brazilian providers.
7. Competitive Landscape
Cialne mentioned speaking with Celonis earlier that day for similar automation and AI agent services. They are evaluating multiple providers to find the best fit for each specific need in terms of quality, cost, and delivery speed.
