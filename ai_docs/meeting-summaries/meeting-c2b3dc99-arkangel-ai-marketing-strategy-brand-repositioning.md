## Discussion Points

### Brand Audit & Narrative Disconnect
Daniel's brand audit revealed a critical gap between Arkangel's PR narrative (focused on saving lives) and its web positioning (targeting revenue/CFO personas). This misalignment undermines brand coherence and market trust.

### Brand Repositioning Strategy
Proposed repositioning Arkangel AI as the 'Bureaucracy Slayer' with an Iron Man archetype — projecting innovation, audacity, and real impact rather than generic corporate messaging.

### Content Engine (QMD)
José presented the internal Content Engine (QMD), an already-developed content generation and distribution system. Tangible results from this engine are expected within 3 months of implementation.

### Commercial Model & Pricing
Revenue share model adjusted to 6-9 month cycles to match enterprise sales timelines. Enterprise pilot minimum set at >$60K, with a tiered offer scale of $99, $1K, and $25K.

### Authenticity Over Corporate Theater
The team explicitly rejected superficial corporate activities (events, vanity metrics) in favor of authentic, value-driven marketing that reflects the founders' real identity and mission.
