# Meeting Summary: Budget & Project Management Workflow

**Date:** 2026-03-04
**ADW ID:** fec51050

## Summary

Oscar and Daniel Cardona discussed how incoming projects are managed using Monday.com for task assignment and tracking. Daniel explained the quoting process, including prioritization, established checklists for specialized equipment, and how the team distributes information and compiles project files. They reflected on how detailed design reviews were a key competitive advantage that attracted their first major clients, but acknowledged that the current volume of incoming projects has made it difficult to maintain that level of review.

## Participants

- Oscar (Supervisor / Project Reviewer)
- Daniel Cardona (Project Manager)

## Discussion Points

### Design Review as Competitive Advantage
The team historically differentiated themselves by providing detailed design reviews to clients, identifying norm non-compliance, incorrect quantities, and pricing errors. This generated significant perceived value and attracted their first major clients. Due to current workload volume, this practice has been discontinued.

### Project Assignment Workflow via Monday.com
Each team member manages their own projects. When a new project arrives, it is assigned via Monday.com by creating a category entry and tagging the responsible person with a comment. Oscar reviews completed work afterward.

### Quoting and Budgeting Process
The assigned person evaluates project priority and determines what needs to be quoted. For specialized equipment, a pre-established checklist guides the process. The team distributes information, compiles the project file, collects supplier quotes, and then reviews pricing.
