Oscar highlighted that reviewing construction plans and RFIs (Request for Information) before quoting adds significant value to the proposal — not just pricing, but advising where costs can be optimized. Accelerating this process is a key deliverable.

Multiple departments need process improvements: engineering, SCT, human resources, and public projects. All areas have processes that could benefit from automation.
